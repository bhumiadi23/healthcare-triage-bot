🚦 Triage System

4-Level Care Routing

Inspired by the Emergency Severity Index (ESI). Each level has a defined action, validated by rule engine and KG.

🚨 CRITICAL — Score 90–99
Call 911 / 108 IMMEDIATELY. Life-threatening emergency. No delay tolerated.
Examples: MI, Stroke (FAST), Meningitis, SAH, Sepsis
⚠️ HIGH — Score 70–89
Go to Emergency Room NOW. Evaluation within 30 minutes.
Examples: Isolated chest pain, Dyspnea, Abdominal pain + fever
⏱ MEDIUM — Score 30–69
Visit urgent care within 4 hours. Monitor for escalation.
Examples: Moderate fever, Cough, Headache, UTI symptoms
✓ LOW — Score 0–29
Schedule GP appointment within 48–72 hours. Home management guidance.
Examples: Common cold, Mild headache, Runny nose
🔒 Safety Guarantee: The rule engine's 30+ hardcoded red-flag rules ensure that CRITICAL combinations (like chest pain + shortness of breath) can never be output as LOW or MEDIUM — regardless of LLM output.
